Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionallly filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total:Approximately 10 minuts
  • Preparation:5 minuts
  • Cooking:5 minuts

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to tsate
  • Pepper, to tast
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional filling: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meat, herbs.

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs:In the bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixwd.You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffier texture.
  2. Heat the pan:Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter oil.
  3. Cook the omelette:Oonce the butter is melted and bubbling,pour in the eggs.Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.
  4. Add filling (optional):When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly runny in the middle, sprinkle your chosen fillings over one half of the omelette.
  5. Fold and serve:As the omelette continues the cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings.Let it cook for another minute,then slide it onto a plate.
  6. Enjoy:Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if nedded.
